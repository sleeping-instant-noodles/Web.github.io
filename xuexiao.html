My School

Zhejiang Normal University is an academic powerhouse in Zhejiang Province. Its history can be traced back to the 1950s. After years of precipitation, it has a rich cultural heritage. The university enjoys a strong faculty, with numerous professors and scholars who have achieved fruitful results in their research fields and are enthusiastic about teaching, thus facilitating the growth of students. It features a diverse range of disciplines, covering liberal arts, sciences, engineering, education, arts, etc., with traditional advantageous disciplines standing out and emerging disciplines showing great potential for development. On the campus, there are modern and advanced teaching buildings and laboratories. The library is rich in collections, and the sports facilities are fully equipped. Adhering to an open concept, the university has established close cooperation with many renowned universities around the world, expanding the international vision of both teachers and students. Over the years, its graduates, with their solid professional qualities and excellent comprehensive abilities, have entered various fields and received wide acclaim, continuously adding luster to the university and helping it march steadily towards higher peaks.
