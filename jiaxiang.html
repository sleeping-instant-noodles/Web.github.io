My Hometown

Anyang, Henan is a prefecture-level city in Henan Province with unique features. Here is a brief introduction: Geographical Location Anyang is located in the northernmost part of Henan Province, at the junction of Shanxi, Hebei and Henan provinces. It is backed by the Taihang Mountains in the west and faces the North China Plain in the east. The Zhang River and the Huan River wind through it. Such a geographical location makes it a strategic location and a regional transportation hub, occupying a key position in commercial exchanges and cultural communications. Education The basic education has achieved remarkable results and has cultivated many high-quality primary and secondary schools. In the field of higher education, Anyang Normal University performs outstandingly. Its normal majors have cultivated a large number of educational talents. Vocational education fits the local industries and transports practical technical experts, providing a full range of support for the development of Anyang. Generally speaking, Anyang has a profound history, a prosperous economy, a colorful culture and convenient transportation, fully showing the new charm of the ancient capital and attracting the attention of all walks of life.
